    Get to know me!

    I’m Ankit Khatiwada- a creative and detail-oriented frontend developer from Nepal. I specialize in building

    clean, responsive websites using HTML and CSS, and I’m currently expanding my skills in JavaScript and

    React.js.     My journey into web development began with a simple curiosity about how websites work, which

    quickly turned into a passion for creating beautiful and user-friendly digital experiences. I enjoy turning

    ideas into functional and visually appealing websites, with a strong focus on simplicity, structure, and

    mobile responsiveness. I believe a great website should not only look good but also be fast, accessible, and

    easy to navigate. Outside of coding, I spend my time learning new tools, exploring design trends,

    and helping others understand the basics of frontend development. I'm always open to new opportunities,

    collaborations,     and creative challenges that help me grow and improve as a developer.
